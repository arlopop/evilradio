One of my greatest passions in life is music. Whether it be creating, discovering, or simply listening, there are few things I'd rather be doing. My show on Aggie Radio has given me an outlet to express and share this passion. I love to listen to all types of music, so naturally Evil Radio has no specific focus, but I like to select creative themes each week. I like to help people feel emotion through music. Whether you feel happy, sad, or excited during my show, I know I'm doing my job correctly. I also like to talk quite a bit; I think it helps people connect to me and my show (and maybe get a couple laughs out). Even though it's called Evil Radio, there is nothing but love that I feel towards my listeners and the music, and I like to think I show it to both of them through my show.
